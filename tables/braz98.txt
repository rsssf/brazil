Rules: We have 6 groups of 4 clubs in the First Stage. They play round
robin in groups and the best 4 of each group qualify to the second Stage.
The bottom six clubs of this stage will be relegated. In the Second Stage
we have Playoffs (Best of 3 games in 8 groups of 2). Winners qualify to
third Stage where they will be divided in two groups of 4. Round robin
inside the groups. Top 2 from each group qualify to the Final Stage. Round
robin to decide the Champion and the 2 promoted clubs.
