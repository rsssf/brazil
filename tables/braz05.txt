On September 23rd, it was disclosed that referee Edílson Pereira de Carvalho was involved with internet lottery
gamblers and that he had been bribed to influence the result of some matches in the Brazilian championship.
On October 2nd, STJD (Supreme Sports Justice Tribunal) decided to annul all the 11 matches in which Mr. Carvalho was
the referee and ordered them to be re-played.

NB: Internacional obtained an injunction at a Porto Alegre court against
    CBF declaring Corinthians officially champions (without the replayed
    matches, Internacional would have claimed the title)
