## Brazil 1997


The two teams relegated last season, Fluminense and Bragantino, will
be kept in the first division, which will play with 26 teams this
year.  Atletico Paranaense will start with -5 points because of
the corruption scandal.  Teams will play 25 rounds, top-8 will
qualify.Bottom 4 are relegated. The 8 clubs are divided into 2 groups of 4
in the Second Stage. Teams play each other in round robbin inside the
groups. Winners of each group qualify to final. Final in two legs.
